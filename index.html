Preventing VTE

Help to Prevent DVT
with this simple App from
Oxford University Health

This app explains how the risk of developing a Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) and Pulmonary Embolism (PE) can be reduced. It does not replace explanation and information given to you by our staff but we hope you will find it a helpful guide to use before, during and after your stay in hospital.
